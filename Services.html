Frequently Asked Questions

What is a typical project timeline?

Timelines vary depending on project complexity. A standard web application MVP typically takes 8-12 weeks, while enterprise ERP systems or complex mobile apps may require 4-6 months of development.

How do you estimate cost?

We provide transparent pricing based on project scope, required resources, and technical complexity. We offer both fixed-price contracts for well-defined scopes and time-and-materials arrangements for agile ongoing development.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive SLA-based maintenance packages that include security patching, dependency updates, performance monitoring, and bug fixes to keep your software running smoothly post-launch.

Can you build mobile apps for both iOS and Android?

Absolutely. We develop both native apps (using Swift and Kotlin) and cross-platform solutions (using frameworks like Flutter) to ensure optimal reach and performance across all major mobile operating systems.
